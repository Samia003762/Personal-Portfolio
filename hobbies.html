Hobbies & Interests

Building LEGO

I enjoy building detailed LEGO sets, especially the more intricate ones. It helps me strengthen my patience, focus and appreciation for small, structured steps.

Collecting Small Collectables

I collect Smiskis, stickers and other small items. I like how expressive and unique they can be, and I enjoy organising them. It reflects my eye for detail and my appreciation for creative designs.

Paint by Numbers

Paint by numbers is one of my favourite ways to calm down after work or studying. It encourages consistency and precision, and I really enjoy seeing a picture slowly form.

Learning New Tools and Platforms

Outside of creative hobbies, I also enjoy exploring new technologies such as ServiceNow, data analysis tools and small coding side-projects. I like to learn through building things, experimenting and testing ideas.
